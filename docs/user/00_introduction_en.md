# 🪐 Welcome to Hecos

Hecos is a **Native Modular Helping Companion System** designed to orchestrate intelligence, tools, and visual interfaces into a unified, privacy-first computing environment.

## 👤 What is Hecos?
More than a simple chatbot, Hecos is an **Agentic Layer** for your computer. Following the "LLM-as-CPU" paradigm, Hecos acts as the master orchestrator, managing memory, specialized tools, and cognitive profiles to assist you in complex workflows—from advanced file management to local code execution.

## 🧠 Core Architecture: Modular & Extensible
The heart of Hecos lies in its **Modularity**. Every function—be it a specialized Vision engine, a Web searcher, or a custom Tool—is a pluggable component. This allows Hecos to grow and adapt to the rapidly evolving AI landscape without legacy constraints.

## 🗣️ Multi-Modal Interaction
Hecos bridges the gap between human and machine through high-fidelity interfaces:
- **Neural Voice Pipeline**: Using low-latency "Push-to-Talk" and advanced TTS, you can operate the system entirely via voice, just as you would with a colleague.
- **Cognitive Personas**: Hecos can swap its "Persona" or cognitive profile instantly, adapting its tone, knowledge base, and tool-access permissions to match your current mission.

## 🖥️ Two Sides of the Same Coin: Console and WebUI
Hecos is a modern system that offers two ways to interact:
1. **Hecos Console (The Heart)**: This is the command window you see when you start the program. It’s where the system "thinks," performs security checks, and lets you manage everything quickly using your keyboard.
2. **WebUI (The Face)**: This is the modern interface you open in your browser. Here you can chat visually, send images, and configure every detail with a simple and intuitive graphic interface.

## 🔒 Privacy and Local Intelligence
Unlike many popular assistants, Hecos puts your privacy first. Many of its features are designed to run directly on your computer (**locally**). This means your data and conversations stay with you, without necessarily traveling over the internet.
